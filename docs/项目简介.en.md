## What This Is

`IELTS Speaking AI` is an IELTS speaking practice platform designed to bring full mock exams, free practice, history review, and intelligent feedback into one reusable system.

It is not just a prototype that demonstrates isolated AI features. It already forms a complete practice loop: topic access, recording, transcription, scoring, result viewing, and history tracking are connected into one workflow.

## Who It Is For

- Learners who want full IELTS Speaking mock exams
- Learners who want focused practice on a certain type of topic
- Learners who want to review transcripts, scores, and improved sample answers
- Developers interested in speech transcription, scoring pipelines, and practice-product design

## What You Can Do Here

- Run complete Part 1 / Part 2 / Part 3 speaking practice
- Use Free Practice mode with topic-library selection or custom prompts
- Experience dynamic follow-up questions and voice examiner playback
- Review scoring feedback, transcripts, and history
- Read the project docs for implementation, UI optimization, and product/design decisions

## Why This Project Matters

The value of this project is not only that it can score spoken answers. It is that it connects the most important moments before, during, and after practice:

- Before practice: users can quickly enter either a full mock exam or free practice
- During practice: the system provides a more continuous and exam-like experience
- After practice: the system offers feedback, sample answers, and history tracking for repeated improvement
