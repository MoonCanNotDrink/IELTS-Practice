## What It Covers

The full mock exam flow is designed to simulate the structure of IELTS Speaking across Part 1, Part 2, and Part 3.

## Expected Flow

- Part 1: short personal and daily-life questions
- Part 2: a timed prompt with preparation and speaking time
- Part 3: deeper follow-up questions based on the earlier topic and answers

## Why It Matters

This mode is useful when users want a more realistic, end-to-end speaking practice session rather than a single isolated answer.
